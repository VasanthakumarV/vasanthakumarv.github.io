# Properties of norms

The norm of a nonzero vector $v$ is a positive number $||v||$. The number measures the "length" of the vector. Every norm must share the following two properties:

$$||c v|| = |c| ||v||$$
$$||v + w|| \leq ||v|| + ||w||$$
